# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

Snowly is a community project built in the open. We're committed to keeping it a welcoming space for everyone — regardless of experience level, background, or which mountain you call home.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive in discussions, reviews, and issues.
- Assume good intent. People contribute on their own time.
- Give actionable feedback. "This doesn't work" is less useful than a reproducible case.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, personal attacks, or discriminatory language.
- Dismissing contributions without explanation.
- Publishing others' private information without consent.

## Enforcement

Violations can be reported to the maintainers via GitHub. Reports are reviewed promptly and handled with discretion. Maintainers reserve the right to remove comments, close issues, or ban contributors who repeatedly violate this policy.
